# Privacy Policy for uStor

_Last Updated: December 19, 2024_

**MyMetrix LLC** (“Company,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use the **uStor** mobile application (“App”) and related services (collectively, the “Services”). By using the App, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Scope and Applicability

This Privacy Policy applies to all individuals who access or use our App. It covers information collected through the App and any related features, functionalities, websites (including [https://ustor.xyz](https://ustor.xyz)), and communications that link to this Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy does not apply to any third-party websites, products, or services not directly provided by us. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of third parties before sharing any personal information with them.

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide, maintain, and improve our Services, ensure security, enhance user experience, and support product development.

### a. Personal Information
- **Account Information:** Email address and any optional profile details you provide.
- **Contact Preferences:** Communication and notification preferences.

### b. Inventory and Storage Data
- **Item Details:** Names, descriptions, categories, and quantities of items you choose to store.
- **Location Information:** Storage unit addresses, identifiers, or other location-based information you provide.

### c. Images
- **User-Uploaded Media:** Photographs or images of your stored items, uploaded and stored securely via Google Cloud Storage.

### d. Usage Data
- **Analytics and Engagement:** Interaction data, such as pages viewed, features used, frequency of access, navigation paths, and session durations. This is collected through tools like Firebase Analytics.
- **Performance and Diagnostics:** Error logs, crash reports (via Firebase Crashlytics), and diagnostic data to improve reliability and troubleshoot issues.

### e. Device and Technical Information
- **Device Identifiers:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, browser type, IP address, and mobile network information.
- **Log Data:** Server logs documenting access times, activity timestamps, and technical events for security and operational insights.

4. **Product Development and Improvements:**  
   To analyze user behavior, feedback, and usage trends; inform feature enhancements; and guide future product roadmaps.

5. **Data Analytics and Business Insights:**  
   To perform aggregated analytics, measure performance, improve user satisfaction, and support strategic decision-making.

6. **Customer Support and Communications:**

7. **Marketing and Promotional Communications (Optional):**

- **Access Controls:** Strict role-based access and authentication procedures.

While we strive to protect your information, no system is completely secure. You acknowledge and accept the inherent risks of data transmission and storage.
